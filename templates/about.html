About Me

This is what I do.

Welcome to my rap blog! I'm Arush Verma, a passionate rap music lover who has been immersed in the world of beats, rhymes, and lyrical mastery for as long as I can remember. Growing up, rap wasn't just music to me—it was a way of expression, a culture that resonated with my soul. Whether it's the poetic flow of classic legends or the groundbreaking sounds of modern rap, I’ve always found inspiration in the way rap tells stories, challenges norms, and brings people together.

This blog is born out of my love for rap and a desire to create a space where I can share my thoughts, reviews, and insights on everything happening in the rap scene. Here, you’ll find everything from deep dives into rap history to discussions about the latest albums, artists, and trends. My goal is to celebrate the art form and its global influence, while also connecting with other rap fans who share my passion for this unique genre.

Through this blog, I aim to bring authentic, raw, and insightful content to rap lovers everywhere. Whether you’re a seasoned listener or new to the genre, I hope this space offers something for everyone—because, at the end of the day, rap is for everyone who understands the power of rhythm and words. So stick around, explore, and let's vibe together! tempora!
